Badges!

Cool, so you know when you open up your gmail and it's like "OMG YOU HAVE 284814 UNREAD EMAILS!"

(Maybe you don't know because you're more responsible than I am about checking emails...Let's just assume you're not, though.)
Yeah! So that number up there is what's called a BADGE!
Badges are a numerical indicator showing how many items are associated with a link.
How many cat pictures do you need to look at? 124812847

You can also be super fancy and put your badges in a button!

Time for...(dramatic pause)...THE CODE!


 (This one is without button) 
<a href="http://iheartcats.com/">How many cat pictures do you need to look at? <span class="badge">124812847</span> 

					

(This one is with button)
 <button type="button" class="btn btn-primary">THIS R BUTTON <span class="badge">200</span></button>

Additional information is always helpful, right? That's where labels come in!
.label is a class! It contains six contextual classes--let's look at 'em!
.label-default, .label-primary, .label-success, .label-info, .label-warning or .label-danger
We're going to use our "span" friend again to use these bad boys. Wanna see what they do?! ME TOO!

Alerts!

Red alert! Red alert! Alerts are predefined in Bootstrap for easy access--SCORE!

The class this time is .alert, and the contextual classes are:
.alert-sucess, .alert-info, .alert-warning or .alert-danger
Let's look at it!

Success! Indicates a successful or positive action.
Info! Indicates a neutral informative change or action.
Warning! Indicates a warning that might need attention.
Danger! Indicates a dangerous or potentially negative action.



WOW! Pretty colors!





Code time!


<div class="alert alert-success">
<strong>Success!</strong> Indicates a successful or positive action.
</div>

<div class="alert alert-info">
  <strong>Info!</strong> Indicates a neutral informative change or action.
</div>

<div class="alert alert-warning">
  <strong>Warning!</strong> Indicates a warning that might need attention.
</div>

<div class="alert alert-danger">
  <strong>Danger!</strong> Indicates a dangerous or potentially negative action.
</div>
